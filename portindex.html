About Me

Hello Im shakeel ahmed

My name is Shakeel Ahmed, and I am the son of Shabir Ahmed, residing in Lowang Bani, Kathua, Jammu and Kashmir. I am currently pursuing my B.Tech in Computer Science with a specialization in Data Science at ABES Engineering College in Ghaziabad, Uttar Pradesh. I completed my schooling at Govt High Secondary School in Lowang, where I achieved impressive academic results, scoring 87% in my 12th grade and 78% in my 10th grade. My education has always been a priority for me, driven by a passion for technology and a desire to understand the intricacies of data analysis. Growing up in a family where my father is a doctor, I have been inspired by his dedication to service and the impact of knowledge on people’s lives. This background has motivated me to pursue a career where I can leverage data science to solve real-world problems and contribute positively to society. In addition to my studies, I actively engage in various extracurricular activities, including coding competitions and workshops, which have helped me hone my technical skills and work collaboratively with peers. I also have a keen interest in machine learning and artificial intelligence, and I enjoy exploring innovative ways to utilize data to enhance decision-making processes. As I continue my journey in the field of data science, I am eager to apply my skills in practical settings, collaborate on meaningful projects, and ultimately make a difference through technology.
